🔬 Practical Activities

Hands-on. Eyes open.

Maths you can touch.

From measuring shadows to map-making, building polyhedra to running coin-flip simulations — our activities transform abstract concepts into experiences students never forget.

Geometry Probability Trigonometry Statistics Algebra Number Theory Calculus Concepts
01

Real-world measurement challenges

Students measure buildings and slopes using basic tools — applying trigonometry and geometry to genuine problems in their surroundings.

02

Probability experiments with everyday objects

Coins, dice, coloured beads — students run experiments and discover probability rules by watching patterns emerge themselves.

03

Build & explore geometric solids

Constructing nets and folding polyhedra using paper and card brings 3D geometry to life in ways no diagram can match.

04

Data collection & visual statistics

Students gather real data from their classroom or community, visualise it, and draw statistical conclusions — making stats feel relevant.
